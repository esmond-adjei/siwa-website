## âš™ï¸ How It Works

### From farmer observations to AI-powered forecasts

1. **Farmers collect data via the Android app**

   * Daily weather predictions
   * Indigenous indicators (clouds, heat, moon, wind, trees)
   * Rain gauge readings

2. **Data is synchronized and validated**

   * Cross-checked with scientific datasets
   * Cleaned and structured for modeling

3. **AI models are trained**

   * Machine learning learns relationships between indicators and actual weather outcomes
   * Models are optimized for Ghanaian agro-climatic conditions

4. **Forecasts are delivered back to farmers**

   * Localized planting alerts
   * Early warnings
   * Seasonal guidance
   * Climate-smart decision support

5. **The system improves continuously**

   * Every new data point strengthens forecast accuracy
